Enabling & Disabling mods

Enabling mod(s)

To enable a single mod, you just have to double-click on the desired mod into the mods list. You also can select several mods and go to the "Mods" menu, select "Enable" then "Selection" or by clicking on the "Enable Selected" button. Finlay you can enable all available mods by going to the "Mods" menu, selecting "Enable" then "All".

Disabling mod(s)

To disable a single mod, you just have to double-click on the desired mod into the mods list. You also can select several mods and go to the "Mods" menu, select "Disable" then "Selection" or by clicking on the "Disable Selected" button. Finlay you can disable all available mods by going to the "Mods" menu, selecting "Disable" then "All".
