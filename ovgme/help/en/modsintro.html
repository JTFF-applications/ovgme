Introduction

Once you activated a configuration, the specified mods folder for this configuration is analyzed to gather the available mods, also the backup data are checked to show you the mods you can disable. The available mods and backups are enumerated in the main list view.

The mods list

The mods list show you the available mods and backups for the current activated configuration. The left icon show you the mod status.
: Indicates an available (not installed) Directory-Mod.

: Indicates an available (not installed) Mod-Archive (Zip).

: Indicates an Installed (enabled) mod.
